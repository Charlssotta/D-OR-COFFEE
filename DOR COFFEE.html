Coffee Americano What is Coffee Americano

also known as americano or American, is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso shot with hot water at a 1:3 to 1:4 ratio, resulting in a drink that retains the complex flavors of espresso, but in a lighter way.

Hot Caramel Macchiato What is Hot Caramel Macchiato

It is made with vanilla syrup, steamed milk, espresso, and caramel sauce. The espresso shot is poured on top of the milk leaving a dark mark on top of the milk foam ("macchiato" means "marked" in Italian).

Foamed Espresso What is Foamed Espresso

The foam found at the top of your cup is "crema" comes from the espresso making process. Espresso is prepared in a metal contraption, which you pound down and hot water is pumped through at great pressures. The pressure pushes out the little oils from the coffee beans into the liquid.
